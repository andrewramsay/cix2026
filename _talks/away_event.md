Away Event
A bus from the University to Balmaha on Loch Lomond. Buffet meal and drinks at the [Oak Tree inn](https://www.theoaktreeinn.co.uk/),    and people can stay there or walk along the shore of Loch Lomond, or in the woods nearby. Bus will leave Balmaha at 21:00, and will drop people off at the accommodation and University.
