Away Event
A bus from the University (departing from University Avenue, near the Boyd Orr Building which is the tall building opposite the School of Computing Science) to [Balmaha](https://www.seelochlomond.co.uk/discover/balmaha) on Loch Lomond. Buffet meal and drinks at the [Oak Tree inn](https://www.theoaktreeinn.co.uk/), and people can stay there and enjoy the food and drink or [walk along the shore of Loch Lomond, or in the woods nearby](https://www.lochlomond-trossachs.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/Millenium-Forest-Trail-Balmaha-walking-route.pdf). A waterproof jacket and sports shoes should be enough for that walk (although it might be muddy..).

The bus will leave Balmaha at 21:00, and will drop people off at the accommodation and University.
